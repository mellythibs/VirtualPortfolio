Building My Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio and blog! I’m Melanie Thibodeau, 26, figuring out my career and life. Funny enough, this portfolio was on my resolution list, but it wasn’t the first project I thought I’d start. My boyfriend, Dylan, suggested I make one since a lot of people in our field have one. It gives me a place to build projects and actually showcase them so others can see.

I’m excited to grow this with future projects. I don’t want it to be just another portfolio for recruiters. As I build things, I’ll share updates here in the blog. The project page will have the latest details, but the blog will show my roadmap, progress, and things I find interesting. Plus, it’ll keep me motivated to let my projects shine! I’ve got some exciting projects in mind: AI, animation, and more, but I’ll share those as they unfold. I’m also excited to see who might follow along whether it’s fellow developers, friends, or anyone curious about the journey.
